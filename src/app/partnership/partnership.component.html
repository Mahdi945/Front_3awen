Partnering with Companies

We believe in achieving more together and partner with others who share our vision and values.

LinkedIn

LinkedIn republishes our volunteer and fiduciary opportunities to its members in Tunisia, allowing us to reach over 20 million professionals (an in-kind support valued at over 1 million Dinars annually).

Enda inter-arabe

Enda inter-arabe supports our microfinance and economic development initiatives in Tunisia, enabling thousands of people to access financial resources and growth opportunities (an in-kind support valued at over 500,000 Dinars annually).

Tunisia Charity

Tunisia Charity collaborates with us to provide humanitarian aid and organize volunteer projects for vulnerable populations in Tunisia, touching thousands of lives every year (an in-kind support valued at over 300,000 Dinars annually).

United Nations Volunteers (UNV)

The United Nations Volunteer program with missions in Tunisia and globally, allowing us to reach thousands of volunteers (an in-kind support valued at over 700,000 Dinars annually).

Habitat for Humanity

Habitat for Humanity builds housing for families in need with the involvement of volunteers, improving the living conditions of many people (an in-kind support valued at over 600,000 Dinars annually).

Rotary International

Rotary International conducts community service projects and volunteer initiatives globally, impacting communities worldwide (an in-kind support valued at over 800,000 Dinars annually).
