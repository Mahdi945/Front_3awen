About Us

We Empower Charities to Reach Those in Need

3awen is a dedicated intermediary society committed to connecting charities with communities in need around the world. Our goal is to streamline support and provide essential resources efficiently and compassionately.

Mahdi Bey, Founder

3awen collaborates with various charitable organizations to deliver aid directly to those who need it most, from basic necessities to educational and healthcare support. We believe in building a bridge of hope and creating lasting change by mobilizing resources to reach underserved communities efficiently and effectively.
